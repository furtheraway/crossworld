CrossWorld

-- to Know, to Understand, and to Collaborate with one another

  • This is a special weekly "social hour", where you step out of your tribe and meet poeple with different backgrounds.

  • Here, we form small-table interactive conversations (2-3 person), with great patience for one another.

  • Interdisciplinary skills sharing/exchange is highly encouraged to promote more accessible collaborations.
